OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH

Occupational health Risk prevention
Blows/cuts by work tools
  • Buy safe machines and tools
  • Keep work material well kept
  • Tools must be in good condition
  • Wear protective equipment (gloves, helmets, etc.)
Incencios
  • Extintores
  • MMantenimiento de los extintores
  • Revisar las intal·laciones electricas
  • Instalar sistemas de detección y alarmas
  • Señalización/li>
  • Realizar planos de emergencia
Fires
  • Temperature between 17º and 27º
  • Installation of air conditioning systems
Visual fatigue
  • Brightness graduation on screens
  • Correct placement of the screens
Electric risk
  • Use tools to prevent the risk of electricity
  • Avoid electricity overload
  • Perform maintenance of electrical installations
  • When operating with equipment, check that it is disconnected from the current
